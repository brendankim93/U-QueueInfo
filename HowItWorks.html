How It Works

keyboard

Add Customer To Waitlist

1) Customers can add themselves on the waitlist either on the restaurant's POS system or if they are in the vecinity they can use the app. They must write down their name, party size, and phone number in order to be added.

assignment_turned_in

Waitlist Confirmation

2) Customers will then receive a text confirming that they have been added to the list. They can then see the estimated wait time to be seated.

local_activity

Reward Points System

3) Customers will have a track record of points based on whether they actually stayed and waited until being seated. This incentivizes the customers to want to stay and actually wait the entire time instead of leaving in the middle of their wait.

tablet

Final Notification

4) When the seat becomes available, the customer will be notified via text. The customer will remain on the list until the hostess has confirmed their seating.
